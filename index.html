Teacher-Subject Hour Allocation

Optimize teacher hours across subjects with constraints

Problem Description

Goal: Optimally allocate teacher hours to subjects, prioritizing important subjects while respecting capacity constraints.

Variables:

  • \(A \in \mathbb{R}^{s \times t}\) — max hours teacher \(j\) can teach subject \(i\)
  • \(b \in \mathbb{R}^{s}\) — required hours per subject
  • \(w \in \mathbb{R}^{s}\) — priority weights per subject
  • \(X \in \mathbb{R}^{s \times t}\) — decision variable: fraction of teacher \(j\)'s capacity allocated to subject \(i\)
  • \(\mathbf{1}\) — vector of ones

Linear Program:

\[ \begin{aligned} \max_{X} \quad & w^\top (A \circ X) \mathbf{1} \\[0.5em] \text{s.t.} \quad & (A \circ X) \mathbf{1} \leq b & \text{(no overbooking)} \\ & X^\top \mathbf{1} \leq \mathbf{1} & \text{(teacher capacity)} \\ & 0 \leq X \leq 1 \end{aligned} \]

where \(A \circ X\) denotes the Hadamard (elementwise) product.
